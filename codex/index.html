Milky Way browse variants

Three isolated versions, all under codex/. The goal is the same in each: make the zoom from lived-in sky to readable galaxy context actually make sense.

V1

Staged

Baseline JSON-driven canvas version with discrete explanatory viewpoints.

  • Fastest to evaluate
  • Most diagrammatic
  • Weakest sense of exploration
Open staged

V2

Cinematic

Scroll and autoplay drive a guided dissolve from immersive sky into local structure and then whole-galaxy abstraction.

  • Best for “make it make sense”
  • Strongest storytelling
  • Less freeform browsing
Open cinematic

V3

Explorer

Hybrid version with semantic zoom stages, minimap, keyboard navigation, and landmark focus.

  • Best interaction model
  • Still keeps explanatory stages
  • Most complex of the three
Open explorer
